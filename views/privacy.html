Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google account ID. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, drive, or any other Google services.

We also store the investment data you voluntarily enter, including ticker symbols, share quantities, cost basis, and account types (TFSA, RRSP, Non-Registered).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Display your personalized dividend tracking dashboard
  • Calculate dividend income projections based on your holdings

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the IncomeMap service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database hosted on Render. Sessions are encrypted and transmitted over HTTPS. We use industry-standard security practices including HTTP security headers, rate limiting, and secure session cookies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The only external services we communicate with are:

  • Google OAuth — for authentication only
  • Financial Modeling Prep API — to fetch stock and dividend data (no personal information is sent)
  • Bank of Canada API — to fetch USD/CAD exchange rates (no personal information is sent)

5. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you signed in. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party cookies.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request.

7. Children's Privacy

IncomeMap is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us at torin.business.video@gmail.com.
